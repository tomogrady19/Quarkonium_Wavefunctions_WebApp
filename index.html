Quarkonium Radial Wavefunctions

This document presents numerical solutions to the radial Schrödinger equation for heavy quark–antiquark systems (quarkonia). The wavefunctions shown below were generated using a phenomenological potential model and normalised for comparison across different quantum states. Each figure corresponds to a different quark configuration: charmonium, bottomonium, and the mixed-flavour Bc meson.

Figure 1. Normalised radial wavefunctions for charmonium (c–c̄). Multiple (n, ℓ) states are shown.
Figure 2. Normalised radial wavefunctions for bottomonium (b–b̄). Higher binding energy produces more compact states.
Figure 3. Radial wavefunctions for the Bc meson (b–c̄). This mixed-mass system exhibits characteristics between charmonium and bottomonium.
